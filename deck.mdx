continuous intergration and either continuous delivery or continuous department

## 持续集成与持续发布、持续部署

---

In software engineering, CI/CD or CICD generally refers to the combined practices of continuous integration and either continuous delivery or continuous deployment. In the context of corporate communication, CI/CD can also refer to the overall process of corporate identity and corporate design.

如何实现DevOps？

<Steps>

- 标准化
- 自动化
- 流水线

</Steps>

---

### 如何标准化？

使用容器技术，在容器中可以保证运行环境在各个阶段的一致性

---

### 如何自动化？

容器技术提供了可受信赖的一致运行环境，这个时候大大简化了自动化部署
借助kubernetes可以很简单的实现自动化

---

### 创建流水线

使用Kuberntes的Tekton Pipeline来自动完成工作流，实现DevOps

---

持续集成服务软件 Jenkins X

Jenkins X provides automated CI+CD for Kubernetes with Preview Environments on Pull Requests using Tekton, Knative, Prow, Skaffold and Helm

---

Jenkins X 涉及太多，这里只讲几个部分

- Prow
- Skaffold
- Tekton

---

### Prow

是Kubernetes项目开发的用户管理Kuberntes开发的一套CI/CD系统

提供了成套的Web Hook来处理任务

可以实现GitOpts，ChatOpts

---

### Skaffold

Google Container Toolkit

开发工具，实现在本地开发，并在集群中运行程序的编译和预览功能

--- 

### Tekton 

云原生的流水线，实现自由配置自动化流水线
